## The club

Rivervalley Rangers AFC has been Swords' community football club since
1981: a youth academy, DDSL boys' and girls' teams from U7 to U17,
senior squads, Over 35s, walking football, and inclusive programmes.
Hundreds of families interact with the club every week — and the website
is where fixtures, news, and club life live.

## The work

This is the project that never ships "done":

- **Match-day hub** — fixtures, results, and club news structured so
  volunteers can keep it current without a developer.
- **Content operations** — the real design problem in community sport is
  who updates the site on a wet Tuesday. Everything is built around
  making that person's job easy.
- **2026 redesign** — a full refresh currently rolling out, carrying
  years of accumulated content forward onto a cleaner, faster platform.

## Lessons

Community platforms age in dog years. The technical choices matter less
than the operational ones: if updating content requires anyone special,
the site is already dying. Design for the volunteer, not the developer.
