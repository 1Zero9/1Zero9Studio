## What it is

ASTRA — Awareness, Security & Threat Response Assistant. Security teams
spend real hours writing awareness content, building phishing
simulations, and summarising cyber events for different audiences. Astra
turns those into generated, editable drafts so the human effort goes
into judgement, not first drafts.

## What it does

- **Awareness content** — campaign copy, posters, and comms tailored to
  audience and tone.
- **Phishing simulations** — realistic simulation content for training
  programmes.
- **Event summaries** — plain-language write-ups of cyber events for
  boards, staff, or technical teams.
- **Prompt Builder** — a standalone workspace for crafting and
  previewing AI workflows in real time, so power users can build their
  own repeatable prompts rather than depending on canned ones.

## Why it matters to me

Astra sits exactly where my interests cross: AI systems applied to
security problems, wrapped in a product that non-technical people can
actually use. The hard part was never the generation — it was designing
the guardrails and workflows so output lands ready-to-edit rather than
ready-to-regret.
