## The brief

QualFM provides facilities and maintenance services across Ireland. Like
most trade businesses, its website has one job: make it easy for the
right customer to understand the services and get in touch — quickly, on
a phone, probably from a job site.

## The approach

- **Services first** — the offering laid out plainly, without agency
  theatre.
- **Trust signals that are true** — real coverage, real capabilities,
  no stock-photo handshakes.
- **Fast and boring on purpose** — a site like this earns its keep by
  loading instantly and never getting in the way of a phone call.

Small business sites are a discipline of restraint: every element that
doesn't help someone hire the company is decoration, and decoration
costs conversions.
