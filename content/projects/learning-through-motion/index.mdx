## The brief

An education platform that needed to look and read like the quality of
the teaching it represented — and needed it in five weeks, not five
months.

## The approach

Tight scope, decided early:

- **Messaging first** — before any pixels, the story: who this is for,
  what changes for them, why it works. Copy drove the design, not the
  other way round.
- **Art direction** — a calm, confident visual language appropriate for
  an education audience; nothing loud, nothing template-shaped.
- **A fast build** — Next.js with content structured for easy editing,
  so the platform could keep evolving after handover without a developer
  in the loop for every change.

## Lessons

Five weeks is enough time to do good work if you spend the first one
deciding what *not* to build. The projects that ship on time are the ones
where scope was a design decision, not an accident.
