## The idea

Every household runs an invisible job queue — and usually one person
carries it in their head. JobJar makes the queue visible: jobs go in the
jar, everyone can see them, and they get shared out simply.

## Why build it

The best product experiments come from problems you live with. JobJar is
deliberately small: no gamification, no streaks, no notifications
begging for attention — just a shared, honest list of what needs doing.
Small tools that respect their users are a design statement of their
own.
