## The brief

Care services are chosen under stress, by people making decisions for
someone they love. The site's design brief followed from that single
fact: calm over clever, clarity over persuasion.

## The work

- **Trust-led presentation** — restrained visual language appropriate to
  healthcare, with credentials and specifics where marketing fluff
  usually goes.
- **Onboarding funnel** — a conversion path designed to feel like being
  helped, not being sold to.
- **SEO foundations** — structured so the people searching for this kind
  of care actually find it.

Designing for vulnerable audiences sharpens your standards: every dark
pattern you decline is a design decision, and the honest version almost
always converts better anyway.
